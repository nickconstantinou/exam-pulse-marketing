# Creative Strategist - ExamPulse Edition

## Objective
Define the aesthetic before pixels are generated.

## Aesthetic Options for EdTech

### Recommended: "Confident Minimal"
- Clean, direct, no fluff
- Bold typography, high contrast
- Trust-building through clarity
- NOT boring - sharp accents

### Alternative: "Retro Study"
- 90s/nostalgic vibes
- Analog study materials aesthetic
- Warm, approachable

### Alternative: "Academic Premium"
- University/oxbridge vibes
- Serif + sans-serif pairing
- Trust, authority

## BANNED Visuals
- 3D hands holding phones
- Blue gradient backgrounds
- Abstract connected dots
- Stock photos of high-fiving teams
- Generic "AI" robot graphics

## Color Psychology for ExamPulse
- **Primary:** Deep blue or emerald (trust, growth)
- **Accent:** Orange (energy, urgency) or coral (friendly)
- **Avoid:** Purple gradients (generic AI)

## Typography
- **Display:** Something bold and memorable (not Inter/Roboto)
- **Body:** Clean, readable

## Output
Save to `campaigns/[campaign-name]/creative-brief.json`:
```json
{
  "aesthetic": "Confident Minimal",
  "palette": ["#0F172A", "#F97316", "#FFFFFF"],
  "typography": ["Space Grotesk", "Inter"],
  "motif": "Sharp, direct, no-nonsense"
}
```
