# Lead Magnets Skill

## Current Lead Magnet
- **Revision Type Quiz** - "What's YOUR revision type?"
- Flow: Landing → Quiz → Result → Email capture

## Alternative Lead Magnets

### 1. Free Gap Diagnostic (High Value)
**Hook:** "Find YOUR gaps in 5 questions"
- Similar to quiz but focuses on identifying weak areas
- More product-forward (shows ExamPulse value immediately)
- Email gates the full gap analysis

### 2. Past Paper Pack ( evergreen)
**Hook:** "3 real GCSE papers with marking schemes"
- No AI, just valuable content
- Good for SEO, evergreen
- Lower conversion but higher trust

### 3. Grade Calculator (Engaging)
**Hook:** "What's your predicted grade?"
- Enter current grades + target → see what's needed
- Fun, shareable
- Email gates detailed analysis

### 4. "5 Myths Killing Your Grades" PDF
**Hook:** "The 5 revision myths that are actually hurting your grades"
- Listicle style, highly shareable
- Positions ExamPulse as the solution
- Email gates the "fix"

### 5. 7-Day Revision Plan
**Hook:** "Your free 7-day GCSE revision plan"
- Day-by-day structured plan
- Email delivers the plan
- Good for Habit-Builders

## A/B Test Recommendations

| Test | Variant A | Variant B |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Lead Magnet | Quiz (fun) | Gap Diagnostic (practical) |
| CTA | "Find My Type" | "Find My Gaps" |
| Social Proof | "2,000 students" | "94% improved grades" |

## Implementation

To build a new lead magnet:
1. Choose the format (quiz, calculator, PDF, email sequence)
2. Write the hook (max 8 words)
3. Create the asset (HTML page, PDF template, email sequence)
4. Test with 2-3 variants

## Metrics to Track

- Conversion rate (visitor → email)
- Share rate (email → social)
- Downstream conversion (email → sign up)
