Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ExamPulse Tutors

How much do tutors cost?

Tutor rates vary based on experience, subject, and location. Typical rates range from £25-60/hour for GCSE-level tutors. A-Level tutors often charge £35-70/hour. All tutors set their own rates.

How are tutors verified?

All tutors undergo ID verification and background checks. We verify qualifications, teaching experience, and references before publishing their profiles. Look for the "Verified" badge on tutor profiles.

Can I meet tutors before committing?

Yes! Most tutors offer a free introductory session (usually 15-30 minutes). This is a great opportunity to meet the tutor, discuss your child's needs, and see if there's good chemistry.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Many tutors offer both in-person and online sessions. Use the location filter on the search page to find tutors who match your preferred format. Online tutoring can be just as effective and offers more flexibility.

What subjects are available?

Our tutors cover all major subjects including Maths, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, and languages (French, Spanish, German). We also have specialist tutors for 11+ and entrance exams.

How do I book a tutor?

Browse tutor profiles, use our search filters to find the right match, then contact them directly through their profile. Most tutors respond within 24-48 hours to discuss scheduling and arrange a trial session.

Is there a fee for parents?

No — using ExamPulse Tutors is completely free for parents. Tutors pay a small fee to list their services. This means you get access to verified tutors without any markup or hidden costs.

What if I'm not satisfied with a tutor?

If a tutor isn't working out, you're free to stop sessions and find another. We encourage leaving honest reviews to help other parents. Most tutors are happy to adjust their approach if you provide feedback.
