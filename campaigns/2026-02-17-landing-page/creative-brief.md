# Creative Brief - ExamPulse Landing Page

## Aesthetic Direction
**Confident Minimal** - Clean, direct, no fluff. Sharp accents, high contrast. Not boring corporate, but trustworthy and modern.

## Palette
- Primary: `#0F172A` (Deep slate - authority)
- Accent: `#F97316` (Vibrant orange - energy, action)
- Background: `#FFFFFF` / `#F8FAFC`
- Text: `#1E293B`

## Typography
- Display: **Space Grotesk** (bold, memorable, modern)
- Body: **Inter** (clean, readable)

## Motif
Sharp, direct, no-nonsense. We're on the student's side. No scary exam pressure, no parental nagging. Just practical help.

## Banned Visuals
- ❌ 3D hands holding phones
- ❌ Blue gradient backgrounds
- ❌ Generic AI robot graphics
- ❌ Stock photos of high-fiving teams
- ❌ Purple gradients

## Vibe
Helpful mate who happens to be really good at exams. Not a teacher, not a parent — a smart friend who's been through it.
