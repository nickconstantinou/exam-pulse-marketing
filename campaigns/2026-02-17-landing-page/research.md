# Market Research - ExamPulse Landing Page

## Pain Points Found

### 1. ChatGPT Gives Wrong Answers
- "Kids who use ChatGPT as a study assistant do worse on tests" (Hechinger Report)
- "ChatGPT got 90% of the answers wrong" (BBC/TikTok)
- Students don't know what's right or wrong
- Trust issue with AI for exam prep

### 2. Revision Overwhelm
- "I'm Sick And Tired Of Revision" (The Student Room)
- Don't know where to start
- Everything feels equally important
- Paralyzed by scope

### 3. Inefficient Past Papers
- Don't know which topics need work
- Spend time on stuff already known
- No adaptation to weak areas
- Boring, repetitive

### 4. Tutoring is Expensive
- Parents stressed about cost
- "Daughter failing GCSEs and I don't know what to do"
- Gap between needing help and affording it
