# Positioning Matrix - ExamPulse

## 4 U's Analysis

### Urgent (Why Now?)
- Exams are getting harder, not easier
- AI is everywhere but students don't trust it
- GCSEs have no second chances

### Unique (Why ExamPulse?)
- AI adapted to YOUR gaps, not generic content
- Trained on real past papers, real exam boards
- Knows when you're getting something wrong BEFORE the exam

### Useful (What Do You Get?)
- Stop wasting time on topics you already know
- Focus revision on what actually moves grades
- Confidence that answers are correct

### Ultra-Specific (Who Is It For?)
- GCSE students (14-16)
- Parents who want to help but can't afford tutoring
- Students who use ChatGPT but know it's lying to them

## Primary Angle
**"Stop Cramming. Start Understanding."**

## The Antagonist
- ChatGPT (confident wrong answers)
- Generic revision apps
- Past papers (inefficient)
- Expensive tutoring

## Market Gap
AI-powered revision that actually knows what's on the exam and adapts to YOUR gaps.

## Hook
"Your revision is inefficient. AI knows why."
